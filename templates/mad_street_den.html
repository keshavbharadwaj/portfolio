Machine Learning Engineer - Mad Street Den

I had the opportunity to work as a machine learning engineer at Mad Street Den, a cutting-edge tech company renowned for its pioneering work in developing AI-driven solutions that redefine the intersection of fashion, retail, and technology

As a machine learning engineer I was tasked with devloping machine learning models to generate tags for an Elastic search-driven recommendation system. Image-based inputs from clients would pass through our machine learning stack, comprising image classification models, OCR for text extraction, and NLP classification models to generate tags such as the presence of tables, geometric shapes, and content-related descriptors based on extracted text.

I engaged with architectures such as CNNs and ResNets for generating vision based tags. For language-based tag generation, I utilized algorithms and models like SVMs and employed LSTMs.

I played a role in crafting indexing and search scripts that utilized the generated tags, and I refined the scoring function to enhance optimization. Additionally, I created and implemented data processing pipelines for client data and tag storage, spanning across Redshift, S3, Dynamo, and Redis databases. My efforts in optimizing the pipeline, by eliminating redundancies and fine-tuning operations, led to a notable 15% reduction in response time.

Location: Chennai,TamilNadu,India

Duration: December 10th 2020 - July 28th 2021
